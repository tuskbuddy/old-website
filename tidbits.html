line game


You play against the computer to cross out 15 lines spread out between 3 rows. The one who crosses out the last line loses.

Before you play: I programmed this in high school and set the AI to be as difficult as possible. The concept makes for a great party game with your friends, but please consider this version more of a willful challenge than a relaxed game.

tunes on piano


I enjoy playing piano and I've made a couple of bits that are each less than a minute long. The first one is fast and has a Latin feel to it. The piano sample I used isn't my favorite, so don't judge me too hard if you're picky about that sort of thing. The second one is a nice jazzy piece, one of my personal favorites. It's short because I don't know how to develop something that strong into a more lengthy piece.
