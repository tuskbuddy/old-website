I'm a senior Mechanical Engineering student from the Midwest. I started coding at 12 and learned CSS, Python, and JavaScript from books, Khan Academy, and rudimentary rock-paper-scissors scripts. Feel free to say hello and drop me a message!

HOBBIES & INTERESTS

I also enjoy cooking, and I'm slowly gathering an army of recipes to seize the means of concoction. I'm limited in what I can eat, but if you have any good recipes that are wheat-free and dairy-free, send 'em my way! I'd be happy to try something new.

I have some Linux experience as well, but not enough to program your refrigerator to play your favorite postmodern grunge album at 3 a.m.

SO, STUFFED CRUST PIZZA

I don't know why stuffed crust pizza isn't more popular. Sweet mozzarella cheese inside soft garlic bread. Delicious pizza on one end, cheesy garlic bread on the other. Mmm. I do understand that pizza crust is a hit-or-miss deal, but does anyone else feel like we're missing out on the glory of eating two different meals with one hand?
