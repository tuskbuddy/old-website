ABOUT ME

I'm a senior Mechanical Engineering student from the Midwest who enjoys geography, Ultimate Frisbee, and black raspberry pie with cold brew coffee.
I use gluten and dairy free ingredients (or make reasonable substitutes) due to some digestive conditions, and I cook for one most of the time, so it's not always easy to find recipes that fit my lifestyle. I also really like chicken. The recipes I've gathered here are a reflection of my endeavors to find delicious meals which satisfy my constraints.


THE BLOG

I wanted to make a website and I wanted to organize my recipes, so I made a website to organize my recipes. Do note that though I say "my" recipes, these recipes come from talented cooks who aren't me and who deserve reasonable recognition. I've linked each recipe to its original page, where you can shower the authors with your finest compliments.
